Game Show: Jeopardy! Battle Arena

Introduction

Welcome to our Game Show: Jeopardy! Battle Arena — a fast-paced, competitive quiz game where 2–4 players face off across multiple categories and rounds. Each round gives players the opportunity to choose questions, place strategic bets, and demonstrate category mastery.

How to Play

  1. Click Start Game to begin a new session.
  2. On your turn, select a category and a question value ($10–$50).
  3. Correct answers award the question's points.
  4. Incorrect answers pass control to the next player; strategic betting may change the points at stake.
  5. After all rounds are complete, the player with the highest score wins.

Tips

  • Prioritize categories you know well to build an early lead.
  • When behind, use higher-value questions or bets to close gaps quickly.
